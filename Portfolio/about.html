About.

"Let me introduce myself through the various facets that make up my unique identity."

I took my first step into the world of professional exploration in 2016 when I proudly graduated from Hacettepe University's Nutrition and Dietetics program. Since then, my journey has been one of compassion and guidance, as I've dedicated myself to the noble field of dietetics. As a dietitian, I've had the privilege of assisting individuals in making healthier choices and embracing balanced lifestyles. With every interaction, my commitment to their well-being grows stronger, and their successes become my own.

The dawn of my fascination with programming brought a new chapter to my story. I embarked on this digital odyssey, diving into the intricacies of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, React, jQuery, and Bootstrap. What started as curiosity soon became a passion. Coding became a language of innovation, allowing me to shape ideas into tangible digital solutions. The more I learned, the more I yearned to learn further. Each challenge and achievement fueled my dedication to continuous growth, inspiring me to remain open to new knowledge and emerging technologies.

Beyond my screen and code, I am an ardent animal lover. Their innocence and loyalty resonate deeply with me, shaping my commitment to advocating for their well-being. The simple joy of being in the presence of animals uplifts my spirit and reminds me of the importance of compassion in our world.

Sports are another realm that captures my heart. From the swift elegance of tennis to the team synergy in basketball, the adrenaline rush of Formula 1 to the precision of dart throwing – I'm an enthusiast of diverse athletic pursuits. Following these sports is not just a hobby; it's a source of inspiration that teaches me about discipline, resilience, and the pursuit of excellence.

My love for creativity finds its expression in painting. Through vibrant strokes, I convey emotions and stories that words often fail to capture. This artistic outlet is my sanctuary, where I can freely explore my imagination and connect with the world in a unique way.

When it's time to unwind, the world of gaming beckons. Both as a player and an observer, I immerse myself in the realm of video and tabletop games. The strategic thinking and immersive storytelling of these games captivate my mind and provide a dynamic balance to my endeavors.

In summary, my journey is an intricate fusion of nutrition expertise, coding prowess, animal advocacy, sports passion, artistic expression, and gaming fascination. These facets converge to create a rich mosaic that defines my identity. As I navigate this path, I am resolute in my determination to further excel in the world of programming, constantly evolving my skills, and embracing the challenges and triumphs that lie ahead.

Thank you for joining me on this journey of exploration and growth.

Warm regards,

Ceyda Ersöz
